Privacy Policy

Knots 22  ·  Nautical 22 Labs  ·  Last updated: 8 May 2026

Knots 22 ("the App") is a dive logging and planning tool for iOS built by Nautical 22 Labs ("we", "us"). This policy explains what information the App handles, where it is stored, and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect or transmit personal information to any server. All data you enter remains on your device. This includes:

  • Dive logs — site name, location, date, depth, duration, water temperature, gas mix, and notes
  • Dive profile samples — time/depth data recorded or imported from a dive computer
  • Photo moments — photos you attach to a dive log entry
  • Marine life sightings — species names and counts you record in the Life List
  • App settings — unit preferences (metric / imperial)

2. Bluetooth

The App uses Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) to communicate with a compatible dive computer. The connection is local and peer-to-peer. No Bluetooth data is transmitted to us or any third party.

3. Photos and Camera

When you attach a photo to a dive log, the App reads that image from your device's photo library or camera. Photos are stored only inside the App's private storage on your device and are never uploaded.

4. Data Storage

All dive data and settings are stored locally in a SQLite database inside the App's sandboxed storage on your device. This data is included in standard iOS device backups (iCloud Backup or iTunes/Finder) if you have backups enabled. We have no access to your backups.

5. Data Sharing

The App does not share, sell, or transmit your data to any third party, advertising network, or analytics service. The only outbound action is when you explicitly choose to export or share a dive card image using the iOS share sheet — at that point iOS standard sharing applies and is entirely under your control.

6. Analytics and Tracking

The App contains no analytics SDK, no crash-reporting service, no advertising framework, and no tracking of any kind.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. Because no data is collected at all, there is no risk of unintended collection from any age group.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after any change constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at nautical22nmi@gmail.com.
